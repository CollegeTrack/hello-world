Poetry Slam (Monday)

In this workshop we will explore and introduce skills that will be used to help promote the use of expression through poetry and deepen your understanding and appreciation of poetry in written and spoken word.
Workshop Goals: Students use spoken, written, and visual language to accomplish their own purposes (learning, enjoyment, expression in a safe and encouraging environment) Students develop self confidence and a sense of community. Practice in and improvement of critical and analytical thinking and discussion skills.

Inside Track: Sisterhood (Tuesday)

In this workshop, young female students will discuss issues, concerns, themes, topics, and experiences among each other to encourage and inspire one another to become more confident leaders among their peers, family, and communities.
Workshop Goals: Create a safe outlet for young girls Promote the agenda of female self confidence Empower and create a greater sense of unity among females Develop stronger bonds and have fun

Inside Track: Brotherhood (Thursday)

This workshop is to gather male students together and talk with others about issues that affect young men today, such as managing responsibilities, healthy relationships, and fostering positive friendships among other young men. This workshop will also engage great young men to develop into greater young men in a safe and fun environment.
Workshop Goals: Increase self esteem Develop a greater significance of the male role in our community Increase awareness of community among brethren.

Improv Theater (Tuesday)

Workshop Goals: Increased confidence Improved public speaking skills Enhanced creative thinking skills Team development skills

Yoga/Mindfulness (Tuesday)

Workshop Goals: Students will, hopefully, develop more learned “acceptance” attitude. Students will, hopefully, gain a higher level of self-awareness.

Class Assignment

1. Decide on palette, typography, and layout for the site

Palette should be two main colors and an accent color.
Typography should be easy to read
Layout should utilize white space for clarity
Use images, text, video, audio, and links to other sites

2. Decide on content for your class home page

Content should include:
Info about the semester and its workshops, e.g., "Keep Your Dreams Alive"
Links to your workshop pages and personal pages

3. With a partner, build a workshop page

Use the site palette, typography, and layout design
Content should include:
Bio and photo for instructor
Class photo and names of students
Workshop description and goals
Quotes from kids and instructor
Use images, text, video, audio, and links to other sites

4. Build your personal page

Decide on your own palette, typography, and layout
Content should include:
“Five things about me”
“What I learned from this workshop”
"What I'd like to learn next"
Use images, text, video, audio, and links to other sites
