HTML Examples || CSS Examples || Class Assignment

1. Decide on palette, typography, and layout for the site

Palette should be two main colors and an accent color.
Typography should be easy to read
Layout should utilize white space for clarity
Use images, text, video, audio, and links to other sites

2. Decide on content for your class home page

Content should include:
Info about the semester and its workshops, e.g., "Keep Your Dreams Alive"
Links to your workshop pages and personal pages

3. With a partner, build a workshop page

Use the site palette, typography, and layout design
Content should include:
Bio and photo for instructor
Class photo and names of students
Workshop description and goals
Quotes from kids and instructor
Use images, text, video, audio, and links to other sites

4. Build your personal page

Decide on your own palette, typography, and layout
Content should include:
“Five things about me”
“What I learned from this workshop”
"What I'd like to learn next"
Use images, text, video, audio, and links to other sites
